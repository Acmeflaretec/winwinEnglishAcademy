About Us

Located in Kochi, WIN WIN INDO-BRITISH ACADEMY is a distinguished institution renowned for its exceptional English language education and IELTS training programs. Led by K C Thomas, an esteemed educator with over 53 years of teaching experience and a strong academic background including an MBA, LLM, and M.Phil in English, our academy is committed to providing students with the skills and confidence they need to thrive in today's interconnected world. At WIN WIN, we prioritize individualized attention, small class sizes, and interactive teaching methods to ensure that each student receives the guidance and support they need to reach their full potential. Our mission is to empower individuals with the language proficiency and academic prowess required to succeed in their personal and professional endeavors. Through our comprehensive curriculum, expert instruction, and personalized approach to learning, we strive to nurture the talents and aspirations of every student who walks through our doors. Whether you're preparing for the IELTS exam, seeking to improve your English language skills, or pursuing higher education and career opportunities, WIN WIN is here to support you every step of the way.

IELTS Preparation Course

Prepare for the IELTS exam with our intensive IELTS Preparation Course. Led by expert instructors, this 2-month course covers all four sections of the exam - Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking - and provides you with the skills and strategies you need to achieve your desired score. Our comprehensive curriculum includes in-depth practice exercises, mock exams, and individualized coaching to ensure you're fully prepared on exam day.

IELTS Crash Course

Short on time? Our IELTS Crash Course is designed to give you a quick and effective overview of the IELTS exam format and essential test-taking strategies. Teacher Meet Professional Trainer

K C Thomas, with over 53 years of teaching experience and a strong academic background including an MBA, LLM, and M.Phil in English, leads by example as our esteemed mentor and expert IELTS trainer. With over 53 years of teaching experience in various colleges and institutes, our founder, K C Thomas, is a seasoned educator with a passion for English language proficiency. Recognizing the need to help individuals perfect their English skills from the basic level, Mr. Thomas authored the book "Perfect English to Succeed More in Life." "Perfect English to Succeed More in Life" is a comprehensive guide filled with ample examples from everyday conversation. Drawing on his extensive teaching experience, Mr. Thomas has crafted a resource that equips readers, even those with no basic knowledge of English, to speak and write perfect English confidently without the need for extensive grammar learning. As both a teacher and an author, Mr. Thomas brings a wealth of expertise and dedication to WIN WIN INDO-BRITISH ACADEMY. His commitment to empowering students with language proficiency extends beyond the classroom, making him a valuable asset to our institution.
